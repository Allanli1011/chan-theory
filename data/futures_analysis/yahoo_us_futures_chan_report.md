## Interpretation

Chan analysis is structurally applicable when a futures time series forms enough merged K-lines, alternating bi, and at least one zhongshu. Continuous futures are usually the cleaner universe because dated contracts often have short or thin histories. Dated contracts that pass the minimum-bar filter can still be analyzed, but their conclusions are more expiration-specific.
